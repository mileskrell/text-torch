<p>Text Torch lets you shine a light on your texting behavior.</p>

<p>After it analyzes your SMS and MMS messages, you'll be told how you compare to each person you message in several ways:</p>
<ul>
    <li>what percent of the time each of you starts conversations with the other</li>
    <li>what percent of the texts each of you has sent</li>
    <li>how the average length of your texts compares to theirs</li>
</ul>
